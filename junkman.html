JUNK

..........A Tale Of Frustration.........

Characters
Pedro - (The Junkman) A tired old man
Claude - (The Cafe Owner) Inept and rude
Rula - (The Chef) Arrogant and bad mannered
Toby - (The Tourist) Innocent and polite
The Police Chief - A very nasty man
Pasha - Beautiful and sexy

Scene: The Piazza, which is on a tourist island somewhere on the Mediterranean. On one side of the square there is a ramshackle cafe, on the opposite side is the Junkman's stall. Central to the square there is an archway entrance to the piazza. The Junkman is dozing quietly behind his stall as the tourist enters through the archway. He moves towards the stall and picks up one of the items displayed on it

The Junkman: (Rather drowsily) 4O

Toby: ( rather surprised) I beg your pardon?

Junkman: 40. The statuette you are holding is 40

Toby: Oh, oh I see. I was just looking really

Junkman: He who hesitates is lost. You should snap it up quickly before it is sold to someone else

Toby: Ah yes of course but let me at least think about it for a moment or two

Junkman: As you wish.

Toby: I wonder, could you tell me when the cafe opens

Junkman: Maybe 5pm. Claude does not keep strict hours

Toby: Claude?

Junkman: Yes the cafe owner

Toby: Ok, 5pm is not too long to wait. I am very hungry though. Is the food good there?

Junkman: No, it's terrible

Toby: Oh dear. The hotel I am staying at unfortunately does not serve food

Junkman: You must be staying at the Hotel Metropole

Toby: Yes I am.

Claude: (in a rush) Here are your coffee and rolls Pedro. Drink your coffee before it gets cold

Toby: Hello Claude. Pleased to meet you.

Claude: Who the devil are you?

Junkman: This is Toby Claude. He is hoping to eat in your illustrious cafe this evening so perhaps you should treat him a little more politely

Claude: I am always polite but I do not like his over familiarity

Junkman: Perhaps you can see Toby, why the cafe is usually empty.

Claude: I don't do so badly. It is a quiet time of year

Junkman: Anytime of year is quiet for you Claude

Toby: You don't seem to be very nice to Claude considering he just bought rolls and coffee.

Junkman: It is a curious situation Toby. I hate Claude but Claude adores me but hates Sasha, who I love but she hates me but loves Claude

Toby: Yes that is rather curious.

Junkman: Ahh and here is Rula. She hates Everyone.

Rula: (Angrily) I hate nobody! Except that Pig Claude

Claude: Now, now Rula. Calm down.

Rula: CALM DOWN! CALM DOWN he says.

Claude: Look my dear whatever the problem is we can sort it out. I will make you a new offer.

Rula: I spit on your offer

Claude: I haven't made it yet

Rula: Whatever it is I spit on it

Junkman: I had no idea Rula spat so much. I am glad I only have rolls and not soup

Toby: You seem very upset

Rula: And who is this moron

Toby: Errr.... My name is Toby

Rula: Toby smobby, keep your nose out of our business.

Toby: This is not the friendliest resort I have ever been to

Junkman: Luckily you have not caught us on a bad day

Rula: I came back to collect my suitcase. I quit

Claude: Please, please Rula. Think it over.

Rula: I have thought it over and I still quit. You are a rude, imbecilic moron that smells terrible and you could not run a business if your life depended on it

Junkman:I can't help but agree.

Claude: Pedro, Pedro my friend, do not take her side

Toby: You do seem really angry Rula

Rula: That is because I am angry you idiot

Claude: I am sure we can sort something out.

Rula: Men!!! You are all the same. When I worked in Paris I cooked meals fit for a king

Junkman: Maybe that's why they had the revolution

Rula: The men working there could not accept that a woman was such a great chef.

Junkman: Why did they sack you then?

Rula: They did not sack me you disgusting old tramp. I left. With my held held high and my dignity intact. It is unbelievable. They were so jealous that even the waiters in the restaurant refused to eat the free meals that I cooked, they went elsewhere

Junkman: As you say, unbelievable.

Rula: So goodbye Claude I hope I never see you again you miserable excuse of a business man

Claude: Please don't go my dear

Rula: Nothing you can say will make me stay

Claude: Rula, Rula just one moment (Quielty) Is it the wrong time of the month for you my dear?

Rula: (loudly) NO! It is the wrong time of the month for you. ( there is the sound of a face being slapped)

Junkman: That probably wasn't the best thing you could have said Claude

Claude: I have to get to work if I am to open today, eat your rolls.

Toby: (Sadly) It seems the cafe maybe opening late today

Junkman: It seems that way

Toby: Is there anywhere else I can eat?

Junkman: No, so many places have closed since the recession started.

Toby: I cannot believe there is nowhere else at all in this town that serves food.

Junkman: That's the way it is. Nowhere else at all â€“ Apart from the Hotel Splendide

Toby: (Hopefully) The Hotel Splendide?

Junkman: Yes.

Toby: They serve food?

Junkman: Oh yes, the finest food in the land.

Toby: And it is open now?

Junkman: Yes, 24 hours a day

Toby: Then why the devil didn't you tell me before. I will go there right now.

Junkman: There would be no point

Toby: Why not?

Junkman: Because you are staying at the Hotel Metropole

Toby: That is true but surely they will serve me dinner?

Junkman: I am afraid they won't. You have to be staying at the hotel to eat there.

Toby: No problem I will Sign out of the Metropole and move to the Splendide

Junkman: That won't work either

Toby: Why not?

Junkman: They feel that anyone that books into the Metropole has no loyalty to them so they would not let you book in there.

Toby: This is incredulous! No loyalty? It is a hotel not the French Foreign legion

Junkman: It is the way of this small island

Toby: Unbelievable.

Junkman: (Excitedley)Ahh Pasha, Pasha you wondrous being, how you make my heart race when you appear

Pasha: Shut up you dirty, disgusting, smelly old man.

Junkman: Even your insults drip like diamonds from your lips

Pasha: I don't want to hear another word from you. You disgust me. I haven't see you here before, who are you?

Toby: Ah good evening, My name is Toby

Pasha: ( In a sultry voice) Pleased to meet you Toby

Toby: Pleased to meet you too

Pasha: My name is Pasha, I errr.. well I work in this piazza

Toby: Oh I see. In the cafe?

Pasha: Unfortunately not. Although I would if I had the chance. Claude is such a charming man

Junkman: Are we talking about the Claude that I know?

Pasha: Shut up pig

Toby: I am hoping to eat there when he opens

Junkman: Rula Just quit.

Pasha: Oh really, maybe Claude will let me work there now.

Junkman: Of course he won't, he hates you

Pasha: Nobody asked you! - Toby, do you have a cigarette please

Toby: Ahh no, sorry, I don't smoke

Pasha: (With disappointment) Nobody smokes these days. They are all afraid of dying. For some reason they think it will be worse than living.

Police Chief: Ahhh What in interesting little gathering we have here

Junkman: Not that interesting really

Police Chief: I see you are not dead yet

Junkman: Not yet. Still time before the sun sets though

Police chief: Trying to be to clever will be the death of you one day

Junkman: Unlikely, stupidity might be though

Police Chief: And how about you my dear how are you faring today?

Pasha: So so

Police Chief: Only so so? I am sure that you can do better than that. Is this a new client of yours?

Toby: Err no, not at all. I am just waiting to eat at the cafe

Police Chief: I see he is still closed, I came by to get my coffee. I don't like it when I have to wait

Pasha: Rula Just quit so the poor man has problems

Police Chief: His problems, not mine. I will continue my patrol now. When you see him tell him I was disappointed he did not have my coffee ready. Very disappointed

Junkman: Life is full of disappointments

Police Chief: You should know old man. - I will return later.

Pasha: How I hate that man

Toby: He certainly doesn't seem very pleasant

Pasha: So, Mr Toby. Are you going to be my client as the police chief assumed

Toby: Ahhm, I am not sure what you mean by your client

Pasha: Do you want to avail yourself to my services?

Toby: (mystified) Services??

Junkman: It is clear to see you are not a man of the world Toby

Toby: Well I suppose I have led a somewhat quiet life

Junkman: Pasha my dear I think that Toby may be a little too innocent for the type of service you offer

Pasha: Keep your nose out of my business pig

Junkman: I am only trying to help

Pasha: How can a smelly old man like you help. If he does not want my services that's fine. I'll starve tonight

Toby: Oh I am sorry, I do not want you to starve

Pasha: Don't concern yourself with me

Junkman: If you will only listen to me I may have an answer for you

Claude:(angry) What a mess she has made of that kitchen. I am well rid of her

Rula: (Just as Angry) Well rid of me are you Claude?

Claude: Ah forgive me Rula, I am a little flusttered

Rula: I don't give a damn what you are. I forgot my suitcase. I will fetch that and then be gone

Claude: Are you sure you will not reconsider?

Rula: Absolutely sure now out of my way you grubby little man

Claude: I really can't imagine what I have done to deserve all this

Junkman: I can Claude

Toby: Excuse me Claude, do you have any idea when you might open this evening?

Claude: I have told you before not to be too familiar with me. I open when I open. That is all I can say

Pasha: Is there anything I can do to help you Claude?

Claude: You?? - You?? There is nothing you can do to help me with anything you, you, you - woman of the street

Junkman: By the way Claude, the Police Chief passed through. He was very disappointed that you didn't have his coffee ready

Claude: Oh dear, dear, dear yes. I must get back to work

Pasha: So what is your idea you miserable old man

Junkman: It seems Toby does not deire any of your usual services, however, he is very keen to eat at the cafe. Perhaps he could hire you as his dinner guest

Pasha: Well maybe you are not as stupid as you look. That might work. How about it Toby?

Toby: (A little flustered) Ahhm. Yes, yes I suppose I couild do that

Pasha: Good. My fee for that would be 20

Junkman: Do you agree Toby?

Toby: Well yes. But I don't have any cash at the moment. The ATM at the hotel was not working

Junkman: It never works

Pasha: Ok, so then it looks like I shall starve tonight

Toby: Oh please I really don't want you to starve but I can't see what I can do

Pasha: I have an idea now. You could sell something?

Toby: Sell something?

Pasha: Yes. This old goat doesn't just sell junk, he buys it too

Toby: I don't have any junk. I don't come on vacation with a case full of junk to sell in case I need to take someone to dinner

Junkman: That tie you are wearing Toby. It looks like good quality silk. I will give you 20 for it Toby: No, no I cannot sell that, it was a birthday gift from my mother

Pasha: Ok I will starve then

Toby: (Exasperated) Don't make me feel so guilty. I don't want you to starve

Pasha: Don't feel guilty maybe I deserve to starve

Toby: Ok I have an idea now

Junkman: We are great ideas people

Toby: How about you lend me the 20 and you can hold on to the tie as security

Junkman: I cannot do that I am afraid. I have no licence to lend money only buy and sell. If the Police Chief found out I had been lending money I would be in jail before you could blink

Toby: It was just a thought

Junkman: What I could do though is to buy the ties from you then tomorrow when you can get some cash you can buy it back from me

Pasha: Another good idea from the pig

Toby: well that seems to be the answer. Here is the tie

Junkman: And here is the 20

Toby: And Pasha here is the 20 for you.

Pasha. Thank you. So tonight I will not starve. I get a free meal and 20 besides. Come Toby, there is a juke box in the cafe, lets go in and dance

Toby: Oh no, no I really am not much of a dancer.

Pasha: Nonsense, come take my hand and I will teach you to dance

( There are a few moments silence)

Police Chief: Still no coffee for me I see. This is a very sad state of affairs

Junkman: Claude has problems

Police Chief: His problems are really no concern of mine

Junkman: Nor mine

Police Chief: That is a very fine tie you have on your stall. Normally all you have is a pitiful pile of junk

Junkman: (a slight panic) It really is not as good as it looks, cheap silk copy

Police Chief: It looks genuine to me. And a quite attractive pattern

Junkman:Oh that pattern is so last year, nobody wears that pattern any more. And really it is too wide to be fashionable Police Chief: You have been selling junk for too long. You no longer recognise quality. How much is it?

Junkman: I really wouldn't want you to waste your money on it

Police Chief: You have aroused a suspicion in me. You seem very reluctant to sell it which makes me think perhaps you loaned money on it?

Junkman: LOANED? Oh no, no, no. I have no licence to loan.

Police Chief: Precisely. Here is 20 for it and be grateful you are not in jail

(Suddenly there is lots of excited shouting from Claude, Toby and Pasha)

Claude: FITRE! FIRE! FIRE! Quick get water

Toby: The kitchen is on fire, the cafe is burning down

Pasha: Help us put it out, it will burn to the ground

Police Chief: The fire service will take hours to get here, there is nothing you can do

Claude but my cafe! My business, I will lose everything

Toby: I am so sorry Claude. We did try to put it out

Pasha: Oh my gosh! The cafe is almost gone already

Junkman: An old wooden building like that had no chance in a fire

Claude: (sobbing) What am I to do? What am I to do

Toby: Hey that's my tie you are holding

Police Chief: No my tie

Toby: It wasn't for sale

Police Chief: Ah so money was loaned against it

Junkman: Oh no!

Police Chief: I am calling for reinforcements

Toby: Reinforcements? Why

Police Chief: He, is under arrest for lending money without a licence, you are under arrest for loaning the goods, Claude is under arrest for polluting the atmosphere, and she is under arrest for being indecently dressed.

Pasha: It wasn't my fault, my dress caught fire

Police Chief: Tell that to the judge, when you eventually get your trial. A successful day for me!

THE END
